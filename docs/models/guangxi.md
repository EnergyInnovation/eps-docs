The Guangxi Energy Policy Simulator (EPS) is a free and open-source computer model created by [Energy Innovation LLC (EI)](https://energyinnovation.org/) and the [Beijing Smart Green Transport Research Center (Smart Trans)](http://smart-trans.net/). It is adapted from software originally created by EI.

## Model Download

The Guangxi Energy Policy Simulator may be used on this website through your web browser, or the full version may be downloaded to your computer by clicking the button below. Note that you will need to go through the steps explained on the [EPS download page](../download) in order to install the required software and make use of the full version of the model.

<p><a href="https://wkf.ms/4oB6m7m" class="btn">Download the Energy Policy Simulator, Guangxi Version</a></p>
