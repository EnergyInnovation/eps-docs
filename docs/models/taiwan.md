The Taiwan Energy Policy Simulator (EPS) is a free and open-source computer model created by [Energy Innovation LLC (EI)](https://energyinnovation.org/) and the [National Taipei University of Technology (NTUT)](https://www-en.ntut.edu.tw/). It is adapted from software originally created by EI. Model documentation in Chinese is available at [this link]().
